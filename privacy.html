Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to the trust that makes therapy possible. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Better Days Therapy & Wellness, PLLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all individuals who visit our website and use our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any information you voluntarily provide through our contact form.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs. This data is collected in aggregate and is not personally identifiable.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device that help us improve your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule appointments
  • Provide and improve our services
  • Communicate important information about your care
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. HIPAA Compliance

Better Days Therapy & Wellness, PLLC is a covered entity under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). All protected health information (PHI) is handled in strict accordance with HIPAA regulations. Clinical records and session information are maintained separately from website data and are subject to our Notice of Privacy Practices, which you will receive at the start of treatment.

4. Website Contact Form

The contact form on this website is not a HIPAA-secure communication channel. Please do not submit sensitive health information through this form. For secure communication regarding your care, please contact us by phone at (346) 254-7322.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services such as Google Fonts and analytics tools. These services may collect limited usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not share personally identifiable information with these services.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at Send a Message.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
