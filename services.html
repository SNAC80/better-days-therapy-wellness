Our Services

Care That Meets You Where You Are

Every service at Better Days Therapy & Wellness is designed around your unique needs, goals, and pace of healing.

🌿

Anxiety Therapy

Anxiety can feel like a relentless storm — racing thoughts, physical tension, and a persistent sense of dread. Dr. Booker uses evidence-based approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and mindfulness-based techniques to help you identify anxiety triggers, challenge unhelpful thought patterns, and develop practical coping strategies that restore calm and confidence.

☀️

Depression Therapy

Depression is more than sadness — it is a heaviness that can affect every area of life. Through compassionate, structured therapy, Dr. Booker helps clients reconnect with their sense of purpose, rebuild daily functioning, and develop sustainable strategies for managing depressive episodes. You deserve to experience joy again.

🌊

Trauma Therapy

Trauma leaves its mark on the mind, body, and spirit. Whether you are processing a single traumatic event or the cumulative weight of complex trauma, Dr. Booker provides a safe, non-judgmental space to heal at your own pace. Approaches include trauma-informed CBT, narrative therapy, and somatic awareness techniques.

🕊️

Grief Counseling

Grief is not a problem to be fixed — it is love with nowhere to go. Dr. Booker honors the full complexity of loss, whether you are mourning a person, a relationship, a life chapter, or a version of yourself. She walks alongside you through every stage with patience, presence, and deep respect for your process.

💛

Marital & Couples Counseling

Relationships require ongoing tending. Dr. Booker helps couples strengthen communication, navigate conflict constructively, rebuild trust after rupture, and rediscover the foundation of their partnership. Whether you are in crisis or simply seeking to deepen your connection, couples therapy can be transformative.

✨

Wellness Coaching

Wellness is a whole-life endeavor. Through personalized wellness coaching, Dr. Booker helps you clarify your values, set meaningful goals, and build the daily habits and mindsets that support lasting well-being. This service bridges the gap between therapy and intentional living.

🔄

Life Transitions Counseling

Major life changes — career shifts, divorce, relocation, retirement, loss of identity — can destabilize even the most resilient person. Dr. Booker provides grounded, practical support to help you navigate transitions with clarity, self-compassion, and a renewed sense of direction.

❤️

HIV/AIDS Counseling & Support

Drawing on her extensive background as an HIV/AIDS case manager, Dr. Booker provides specialized counseling for individuals living with HIV/AIDS. She understands the unique psychological, social, and medical dimensions of this experience and offers a space free of stigma, judgment, and shame.

Telehealth

Therapy From the Comfort of Your Home

Better Days Therapy & Wellness is a fully telehealth practice. All sessions are conducted via a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform — accessible from your home, office, or any private space.

Telehealth removes the barriers of distance, transportation, and scheduling that often prevent people from accessing the care they need. Dr. Booker is licensed to serve clients throughout Texas and Illinois.
