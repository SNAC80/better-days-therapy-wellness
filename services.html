Healing Options

Healing Options Designed Around You

Every service at Better Days Therapy & Wellness is designed around your unique needs, goals, and pace of healing.

💍

Pre-Marital Counseling

Preparing for marriage is one of the most important steps in a relationship. Together, we’ll address key topics like communication, conflict resolution, and shared goals, ensuring that you’re both prepared for the journey ahead. Using Solution-Focused Therapy and Emotionally Focused Therapy to help couples create lasting, positive change.

💛

Marital Counseling

Every marriage has its ups and downs, and I’m here to support you through it all. We’ll explore the challenges you’re facing and work on improving communication, trust, and connection using Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and Emotionally Focused Therapy to help couples reconnect and move forward.

🌿

Anxiety Management Therapy

Anxiety can feel overwhelming, but together we can develop strategies to manage it effectively. Combining Anxiety Management Therapy with Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to help you identify triggers and build practical coping skills — whether you’re struggling with general anxiety, panic attacks, or work-related stress.

☀️

Supportive Counseling for Depression

If you’re feeling stuck or overwhelmed by depression, I’m here to help. My supportive counseling sessions provide a compassionate, non-judgmental space. Using Cognitive Restructuring, I’ll help you identify and shift negative thought patterns while providing the emotional support you need to heal.

🕊️

Bereavement Support & Loss Adjustment

Grieving the loss of a loved one or coping with significant life changes is never easy, but you don’t have to go through it alone. Together, we’ll work to understand your grief and find ways to navigate this challenging time with compassionate support and practical tools.

🌟

End-of-Life Counseling

Facing the end of life is a deeply emotional experience, whether it’s for yourself or a loved one. I provide end-of-life counseling to help you address fears, regrets, and emotions related to terminal illness or the impending loss of a loved one — with compassion and respect throughout.

🤝

Group Therapy

Group therapy offers a unique opportunity to connect with others who understand your struggles. In my group therapy sessions, I provide a supportive environment where you can share experiences, learn from others, and work on building new skills using CBT and Strength-Based Therapy.

📌

Individual & Personalized Counseling

Personalized counseling is tailored specifically to you and your needs. Using a variety of techniques including CBT, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, and Mindfulness-Based Therapy to help you address your unique challenges and develop practical strategies for emotional growth.

🌺

Stress Reduction & Relaxation Techniques

Stress is a part of life, but with the right tools, it doesn’t have to control you. Through relaxation training, resilience building, and coping skills development, I’ll provide you with the tools to respond to stress in healthier ways and take back control of your life.

📱

Remote Crisis Intervention

Sometimes, life throws unexpected challenges your way. My telehealth crisis intervention service offers immediate support for managing acute emotional distress. Whether you’re dealing with overwhelming emotions or a traumatic event, I provide a safe and confidential space to help you regain control.

🌊

Trauma Counseling

If you’ve experienced trauma, it can feel impossible to heal, but I’m here to guide you through it. My trauma counseling services include Trauma-Focused Cognitive Therapy, Trauma Recovery Counseling, and Post-Traumatic Stress Support, all aimed at helping you process your experiences and rebuild your life.

✨

Wellness Coaching

Life can get overwhelming, and sometimes we need guidance to achieve balance and wellness. In our wellness coaching sessions, I’ll work with you to develop a holistic approach to health, including stress management, goal setting, and emotional well-being — creating a personalized plan to help you thrive.

Ready to take the next step? Dr. Booker is here to listen, support, and guide you toward the healing you deserve.
