Your Path to Healing

Empowering Every Step of Your Journey

Every path to healing is uniquely yours. Choose the support that speaks to where you are right now — and trust that growth is possible from here.

💍

Pre-Marital Counseling

Preparing for marriage is one of the most important steps in a relationship. Build a strong foundation before tying the knot — addressing communication, conflict resolution, and shared goals.

💛

Marital Counseling

Every marriage has its ups and downs. Using CBT and Emotionally Focused Therapy, I help couples improve communication, rebuild trust, and reconnect in a healthy way.

🌿

Anxiety Management Therapy

Combining Anxiety Management Therapy with CBT to help you identify triggers and build practical coping skills — whether you're facing general anxiety, panic attacks, or work-related stress.

☀️

Supportive Counseling for Depression

A compassionate, non-judgmental space to work through depression. Using Cognitive Restructuring to identify and shift negative thought patterns and develop a plan for your well-being.

🕊️

Bereavement & Loss Adjustment

Grieving the loss of a loved one or coping with significant life changes is never easy. Compassionate support and practical tools to help you navigate grief and adjust to life after loss.

🌊

Trauma Counseling

Trauma-Focused Cognitive Therapy, Trauma Recovery Counseling, and Post-Traumatic Stress Support — all aimed at helping you process experiences and rebuild your life.
