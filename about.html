About Dr. Stephanie Booker, LPC, Ed.D

A counselor, educator, and healer with over 35 years of dedicated service to the well-being of others.

Biography

A Life in Service of Healing

Dr. Stephanie Booker, LPC, Ed.D is the founder of Better Days Therapy & Wellness, PLLC — a Houston-based telehealth practice dedicated to compassionate, personalized behavioral health care. With over 35 years of experience across nursing, education, case management, and counseling, Dr. Booker brings a rare depth of perspective to every therapeutic relationship.

Her career began in nursing, where she spent 20 years serving patients in some of the most vulnerable moments of their lives. That foundation of clinical care and human compassion became the bedrock of everything that followed. She transitioned into school counseling, working with young people navigating the complex intersections of identity, family, and academic pressure. She also served as an HIV/AIDS case manager, walking alongside individuals facing stigma, illness, and profound uncertainty with steadfast support and advocacy.

Today, as a Licensed Professional Counselor holding a Doctor of Education, Dr. Booker integrates every chapter of her professional journey into a holistic, client-centered approach to therapy. She is licensed in both Texas and Illinois and provides telehealth services to clients throughout both states.
