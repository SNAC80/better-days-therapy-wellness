About Dr. Stephanie Booker, LPC

A counselor, educator, and healer with over 30 years of dedicated service to the well-being of others.

Biography

Dr. Stephanie Booker, LPC

I'm Dr. Stephanie Booker, a Licensed Professional Counselor, and I'm deeply committed to supporting individuals on their journey toward emotional and mental well-being. With over 30 years of experience in helping professions, including six years as a school counselor, I've had the privilege of working with individuals ranging from 5 to 19 years old, as well as adults facing various life challenges. I earned my master's degree in counseling from Missouri Baptist University, and I'm currently completing my doctoral degree in traumatology.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work with survivors of child abuse, domestic violence, and sexual abuse, as well as parents of children with special needs. I've also worked extensively with individuals experiencing anxiety, depression, PTSD, grief, stress, gender identity concerns, and secondary traumatic stress. Whether in individual therapy or group therapy settings, I strive to create a safe, nonjudgmental space where my clients can explore their emotions, thoughts, and experiences with trust and confidence.

At Better Days Therapy & Wellness, my goal is to help each person realize and achieve their fullest potential in a compassionate, empowering environment. I'm here to listen, support, and guide you toward healing, growth, and a healthier, more fulfilling life. If you're ready to take the next step, I'm here to walk alongside you.

I'm so glad you're considering taking this step toward healing and growth. Whether you're looking for counseling, support, or wellness coaching, I'm here to listen, understand, and walk alongside you.

Mission & Philosophy

The Heart Behind Better Days

🌱

Client-Centered Care

Every person who walks through the virtual door of Better Days is met with unconditional positive regard. I tailor every session to the individual — their history, their strengths, their goals.

🤝

Therapeutic Partnership

Therapy is not something done to you — it is something done with you. I walk alongside each client as a trusted partner in the healing process, never as a distant authority.

🔒

Safety & Confidentiality

Your privacy is sacred. I maintain the highest standards of confidentiality and HIPAA compliance, ensuring that your therapeutic space is truly safe.

🌟

Whole-Person Wellness

Mental health does not exist in isolation. I consider the full context of each client's life — physical health, relationships, spirituality, and environment — in every treatment approach.
