Your First Session

What to Expect

The first session is about connection, not pressure. Here is what the experience looks like.

🏠

Join From Anywhere

All sessions are conducted via telehealth. You will receive a secure video link before your appointment. All you need is a private space and a device with a camera.

📋

Brief Intake Paperwork

Before your first session, you will complete a short intake form so Dr. Booker can understand your background and goals before you meet.

🤝

A Conversation, Not an Evaluation

Your first session is a chance to get to know each other. Dr. Booker will listen, ask thoughtful questions, and begin to understand what healing looks like for you.

🔒

Fully Confidential

Everything shared in session is protected by HIPAA and professional ethics. Your privacy is not just a legal requirement — it is a core value of this practice.

💳

Insurance & Fees

🌿

No Pressure to Continue

You are never locked in. After the first session, you and Dr. Booker will discuss whether continuing together feels like the right fit — the decision is always yours.

Common Questions

Scheduling FAQ

Appointment availability varies, but Dr. Booker makes every effort to schedule new clients within one to two weeks of initial contact. Reach out by phone or email and she will let you know current availability.
No referral is needed. You can contact Dr. Booker directly to schedule. If your insurance requires a referral for coverage purposes, please check with your plan before your first session.
Life happens. Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. You can call or send an email to make changes. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee, which will be discussed during your intake.
Individual therapy sessions are typically 50 to 60 minutes. Couples and group sessions may vary in length. Dr. Booker will confirm session duration when you schedule.
You will need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a working camera and microphone, a stable internet connection, and a private space where you feel comfortable speaking freely. Dr. Booker uses a HIPAA-compliant video platform — no special software download is required.
